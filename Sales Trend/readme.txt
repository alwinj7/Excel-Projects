A Dashboard to find the Sales Trend across the different categories of products.

Month Wise Revenue & Conversion Trends.
Category-wise total revenue along with daily average revenue in the last 10 months.

Exploratory view - Pivot charts & Slicers 
Category Slicer to find: 

Monthly orders and Average revenue per order for the category selected in the slicer.
Impressions and Conversion trend at monthly level.
Pie chart with distribution of total sales across cities.

Calculated Fields used:
To Find Conversions:

Conversions = Orders/Impressions
Avg per order = Revenue/orders
Advanced Functions used: Sumifs, Countifs.
