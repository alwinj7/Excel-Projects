A Dashboard to find the Sales Trend across different category of products

Month Wise Revenue & Convestion Trends
Category wise total revenue along with daily average revenue in the last 10 months


Exploratory view - Pivot charts & Slicers 
Category Slicer to find: 
1. Monthly orders and Average revenue per order for the category selected in the slicer.
2. Impressions and Conversion trend at monthly level
3. Pie chart with distribution of total sales across cities

Calculated Fields used:
To Find Conversions:
Conversions = Orders/Impressions
Avg per order = Revenue/orders

Advanced Functions used: Sumifs, Countifs.
